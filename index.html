About us

We are a sports platform owned by the BP Group, a group that contributes to the development of the Colombian caribbean region. The BP Group is doing this by spreading awareness about good practices when using electronic devices and Information and Communications Technology.

Our platform is a place where you can find information about sports events, sports news, and sports in general. We also have a section where you can find information about the BP Group and its activities.

We hope you enjoy our platform and that you find the information you are looking for.

Founded in 1924 as the Atlántico Football League, it's the representative team of the Republic of Colombia.
